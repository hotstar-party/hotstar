Guide to Hosting Your Ultimate Hotstar Watch Party

Are you a die-hard Hotstar fan who loves binge-watching shows and movies? Then, Hotstar Party is a must-have for you, completely free of charge. Besides, with this extension, you can effortlessly watch your favorite content in perfect sync with friends across the globe. Hosting a watch party has never been easier – follow these simple steps and start enjoying together.

  • Get Hotstar Party Start by downloading the free Hotstar Party extension, which is compatible with Chromebooks, macOS, Windows, and other computers. Moreover, ensure you're using Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge to install this extension
  • Pin the Extension Then, after installing the extension, you'll notice the "Hotstar Party" button in your toolbar—make sure to pin it there. However, if the button isn't visible, click the puzzle icon in the top right corner of your browser and pin it from the menu.
  • Log In to Hotstar Before you can host a watch party, you need to open Hotstar and sign in to your account. Moreover, remember, having an active Hotstar account is necessary to create and join a watch party.
  • Find and Play Afterwards, look up your favorite shows or movies on Hotstar, or choose something new to watch with your friends. Once you've made your selection, hit play to start the fun
  • Host Your Hotstar Party Begin by clicking the "favicon" button to set up your Hotstar watch party. A "CREATE A WATCH PARTY" window will pop up, showing a "START A PARTY" button. Furth, click it to create an invitation URL for your watch party.
  • Send the Invite Link The last step is to share the generated invitation link with the friends you want to join your Hotstar watch party

How to Dive into a Hotstar Watch Party: Your Complete Guide

Hosting a Hotstar watch party is as effortless as joining one. Follow these easy steps to dive into the excitement and join the party

  • Download and Pin the Extension First, download the extension. Then, pin it to your browser’s toolbar by clicking the "favicon" button for convenient access
  • Log In to Your Hotstar Account Ensure each watch party member has their own Hotstar subscription. Once that's sorted, log in to your Hotstar account to get started.
  • Follow the Invitation Link The last step is to click on the invitation link your friend sent. Once you do, you'll be taken straight to the watch party. Now, all that's left is to stream and enjoy with your friends from afar.
